The Hate U Give — Cover Redesign

This project is a contemporary redesign of The Hate U Give, created to better reflect its themes of identity, protest, and social justice. I approached the cover with a focus on bold typography, symbolic imagery, and a clean composition that captures the emotional weight of the story.

MindfullnessOrb

The goal of the Mindfulness Orb is to offer a calm, alternative to smartphone-based wellness, helping reduce reliance on screens for mindful breaks. By providing a tactile, physical interaction free from notifications, distractions, and cognitive overload, the device creates a focused, app-free space for everyday mindfulness, encouraging a more grounded and present experience.
